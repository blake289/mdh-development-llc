MDH Development LLC is a Utah-based commercial real estate development firm led by Marlon Hill. Specializing in ground-up retail centers, master-planned mixed-use communities, and hospitality projects across the Wasatch Front and rural Utah growth corridors.

We have built a portfolio of 45+ projects spanning shopping centers, restaurant pads, office space, auto service facilities, and hotel developments.
